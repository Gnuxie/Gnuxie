Hi, I am maintainer of [Draupnir](https://github.com/Gnuxie/Draupnir),
an open-source community management platform for [Matrix](https://matrix.org),
used by communities to manage abuse and coordinate moderation in a federated environment.

I contribute to the Matrix ecosystem through trust & safety work, MSCs, and through the Matrix [governing board](https://matrix.org/foundation/governing-board/#community-representatives). My work is driven by what communities need now, focusing on systems that help them operate safely and reliably in practice.

You can find my MSC contributions [here](https://github.com/matrix-org/matrix-spec-proposals/pulls?q=is%3Apr+author%3AGnuxie), and you can also find me on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/gnuxie-lulamoon-a4a1b9397).
